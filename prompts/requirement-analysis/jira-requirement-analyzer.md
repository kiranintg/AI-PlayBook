# Jira Requirement Analyzer

## Purpose
Analyze a Jira ticket and extract requirements.

## Prompt

Act as a Senior QA Lead.

Analyze the Jira ticket and identify:

1. Functional Requirements
2. Business Rules
3. User Roles
4. Preconditions
5. Dependencies
6. Assumptions
7. Open Questions
8. Risks

Output Format:

| Category | Details |
