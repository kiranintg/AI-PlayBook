# Business Scenario Generator

## Purpose

Generate complete business scenarios from requirements.

## Prompt

Act as a Principal QA Architect.

Analyze the provided requirements and generate:

1. Primary Business Scenarios
2. Alternate Flows
3. Failure Scenarios
4. Exception Scenarios
5. User Journey Scenarios

For each scenario provide:

* Scenario ID
* Scenario Description
* User Role
* Priority
* Risk Level

Output Format:

| Scenario ID | Role | Scenario | Priority | Risk |
